About Us

A Legacy of Healing Since 1978

Founded by Dr. S. L. Venkatesh in the heart of Vasanth Nagar, SLV Clinic has served four generations of Bangalore families. What started as a small family practice has grown into a trusted multi-specialty clinic — without ever losing the personal touch that defined our beginnings.

Our team of experienced physicians combines time-tested medical wisdom with modern diagnostic technology to deliver care that is both compassionate and precise. We believe healthcare is a relationship, not a transaction.
